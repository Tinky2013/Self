Know More About Me

Where I study and live

I entered Zhejiang University in 2017 and I will study for four years in Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province. Hangzhou, located in the east of China (120°E, 30°N), is 'the land of Internet' and one of the most famous tourist cities in China. Besides, we have witnessed rapid economic development in Hangzhou these years. The G20 was held in Hangzhou in 2016.

Zhejiang University

Established in 1897, Zhejiang University is one of the best universities in China. It has many world-class disciplines and top teaching resources. In 2020 QS University Rankings, ZJU ranks 54th in the world and 6th in Asia.

Chu Kochen Honors College

Chu Kochen Honors College (CKC) is the only honors college in ZJU. The most excellent students from the school will be selected to study in this college, and the last 10% in CKC will be eliminated each year.
